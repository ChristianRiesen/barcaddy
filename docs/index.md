# Barcaddy

A simple, offline barcode and loyalty card wallet.

Stop fumbling through a stack of plastic cards at the checkout. Barcaddy keeps all your loyalty cards in one place on your phone — scan once, then pull up the barcode whenever a cashier asks.

## What it does

- Scan and store any 1D or 2D barcode
- Label and organise your cards
- Show the barcode full-screen for scanning at the till
- Everything stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud, no tracking
- Export as CSV for backup or transfer to a new phone

## Privacy

Barcaddy doesn't collect, transmit, or share any of your data. The only permission it needs is the camera, used solely to scan barcodes when you add a new card. You can also manually enter the details if you want.

Read the full [privacy policy](privacy.html).
